Controls & tips

Move: ← → or A / D, or tilt on mobile/tablet.

Jump: Space or the bottom-right Jump button.

Shoot: Ctrl or the bottom-left Shoot button.

After finishing a level: press Space or tap the game screen.

Hold Space for full jump, release early for a shorter jump.

Avoid red patrol cubes or you respawn at the start.
